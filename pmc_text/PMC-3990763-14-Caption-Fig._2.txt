2D visualization of the entire patient sample. The entire 100 patients in the 2D similarity space with mapping attributes of 1) color → risk stratification (red, very high risk; orange, high risk; blue, medium risk; green, normal; and purple, unknown), 2) shape → gender (O, female; X, male), and 3) bar → status (top-bar, deceived; no-bar, survived). It shows that most of the deceased patients are located in the top-left area.
