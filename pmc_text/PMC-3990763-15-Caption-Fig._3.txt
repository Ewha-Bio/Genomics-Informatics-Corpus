3D visualization of the entire patient sample. The entire 100 patients using the orange-purple color-blind friendly scheme using the same dataset as in Fig. 2. 1) Main colors (spheres) represent year of birth, 2) outer colors (signs) represent risk stratifications (red, very high risk; orange, high risk; yellow, medium risk; green, normal; and grey, unknown), and 3) status (bright outer-bound, deceived; no-out-bound, survived).
