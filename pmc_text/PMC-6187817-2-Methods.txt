Study samples
The first-in-human clinical study enabled exploratory analysis of pharmacogenomics and pharmacometabolomic analysis. The study was approved by the Institutional Review Board of Seoul National University Hospital, Seoul, Korea (H-1011-027-339) and was conducted in accordance with the principles of the Declaration of Helsinki and ICH Good Clinical Practice. All subjects provided written informed consent before any study-related procedure was performed.
Whole blood samples (n = 32) were obtained from every subject who received a single oral dose of drug X for the pharmacogenomic genotyping. Plasma samples (n = 8) were obtained from the subjects who received highest dose of drug X for the pharmacometabolomic analysis.
