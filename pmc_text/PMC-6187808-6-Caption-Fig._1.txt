Odds ratios (ORs) and confidence intervals (CIs) of the LEF1 (rs4365796) association with Kawasaki disease (KD) according to age of Korean KD patients (total number of KD patients, 1,658). Each horizontal bar is a 95% CI. A total of 4,553 controls were used in the genetic association analysis for each age subgroup of KD patients. Significant p-values (p < 0.05) are shown in bold.
